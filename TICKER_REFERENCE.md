## Key Metrics Definitions
- **P/E Ratio**: Price-to-Earnings ratio (lower = potentially undervalued)
- **P/B Ratio**: Price-to-Book ratio (lower = undervalued relative to assets)
- **Dividend Yield**: Annual dividend as % of stock price (higher = more income)
- **Market Cap Classification**:
  - Large-cap: > 300B TWD
  - Mid-cap: 100-300B TWD
  - Small-cap: < 100B TWD
